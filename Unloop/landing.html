Washington Re-Entry Connector

Resources for formerly incarcerated people

The WA Reentry Connector is meant to help people in the state of Washington re-enter their communities following incarceration. This community-owned directory connects people with essential services – housing, education, legal, health, and more – that work with people with convictions. The goal is to help people begin and sustain their lives on the outside. This tool is designed by Unloop and maintained by a network of organizations and volunteers invested in the success of people coming out of prison.

Please help keep these resources updated by flagging and commenting on broken links or outdated service listings. Problem with the tool? Please email info@un-loop.org.
